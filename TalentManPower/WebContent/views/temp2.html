Talent ManPower -- An alternative to hiring employees locally.


  • Want to hire an employee locally? However would like to reduce the cost?
  • Virtual Employees are the better option than outsourcing or hiring freelancers

Hire a Full time Virtual Employee in any profession from India for only $1095-$2995/month e.g. Project Managers, Mainframe System Programmers (z/OS, DB2, IMS, Adabas, CICS, Storage), DBAs (DB2, IMS, Adabas, Oracle, SQL Server), Schedulers, Operators, Software Developers, Accountants, Engineers, Lawyers, Web & Graphic Designers, Data Entry Executives, Transcriptionists, Internet Marketers, Content Writers, Finance Experts etc.

HOW DOES VIRTUAL EMPLOYEE WORK


  • Virtual Employee works directly for you just like a locally hired employee.
  • Virtual Employee works with you remotely from our office in India.
  • And so you work with your Virtual Employee via the internet, instead of face to face.
  • You will be given complete control, you tell your Virtual Employee what to do and you work with them.
  • You review your Virtual Employee’s work and they will follow your instructions only.
  • A Virtual Employee is not a freelancer, however simply a remote employee, dedicated, supervised & professional.

WORKING REMOTELY!


  • Your VE is equipped with the latest computer hardware.
  • You decide what software and hardware your VE uses to work with you remotely
  • Our managers physically your VE to ensure they are working dedicatedly and following your instructions
  • We are present 24*7 should you require any real-time assistance

WE ARE A REMOTE EXTENSION OF YOUR LOCAL OFFICE


  • Think of your office as opening your very offshore office in India
  • You hire a dedicated VE that works exclusively for and with you
  • We act as your physical representatives on the ground in India
  • We manage your employees as you instruct us to do
  • That's not all we take care of all the non-work related issues and office logistics on your behalf.

RESPONSIBLE DATA SECURITY


  • Employee can work real-time on your computer.
  • 24*7 internet traffic surveillance by our IT team.
  • Virtualization and Thin client - employee can work from a cloud environment
  • Removable storage devices on PCs and laptops can be disabled.
  • Websites and personal email accounts can be blocked.

FACILITIES WE OFFER


  • Office located in the heart of National Capital Region of India.
  • Multiple high-speed leased line internet connections.
  • On site 24*7 power back-up & centralized air-conditioning.
  • State-of-the art office facility, high quality infrastructure and spacious work stations.

VIRTUAL EMPLOYEE ADVANTAGE


  • Set up your own office in India at zero cost.
  • Rolling monthly contact - no long term commitment.
  • Hire professionals in any stream - build an offshore team.
  • Time zone difference doesn't matter - your VE can work the same office hours as you.
  • Offshore with control - no middle man - just hire and work with a VE like you would with a local employee.
